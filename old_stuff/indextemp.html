Internet radio has been saved (for now)!

It would seem that the dudes in Congress as well as the sound exchange juggernauts have listened to our pleas and have promised to renegotiate the proposed bone crushing rates. KCPR and internet radio patrons world wide would like to extend their gratitude to anyone who wrote their congress wo(man) or just cared. Even a little bit! Anyways, after we are all done breathing our sigh of relief, we must remember that net radio isn’t entirely saved and many stations could suffer from new and retroactive fees! With this said, the KCPR net feed almost definitely WILL NOT be shut down. Rejoice!

If you would like to learn more about this issue you can check out the passionate people at savenetradio.org or check out this wired news article which sums up the issue really well.
