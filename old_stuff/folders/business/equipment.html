Mobile DJ
KCPR is available to DJ at your event. We'll provide equipment and music and a DJ at a negotiable price. We have DJ's who specialize in various genres of music, from hip hop to trance, punk to country, and all things in between. Contact Jeremy for more information.

Our Mobile DJ equipment includes:
2 Technics 1200 turntables with Numark 3 channel mixer in coffin
2 CD players with 2 channel mixer in coffin
QSC 2 channel amplifier with 540 watts/ channel
2 Mackie S500 3-way speakers with 15" woofers and tripod stands

Sound System
KCPR has its own PA system that is perfect for smaller venues. Our equipment is more than adequate for coffeeshops and bars, and big enough for small halls. We currently have two experienced technicians available to operate the gear.

Our Sound System includes:
Mackie 808M 8 channel mono mixer with built in amplifier at 450 watts/ channel
2 JMF custom 15" monitors
2 Mackie S500 3-way speakers with 15" woofers and tripod stands
Assortment of SM58's & 57's, and other studio mics
Boom mic stands, both solid base and tripod
