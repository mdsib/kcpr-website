Public Service Announcements:
If you have an event such as a concert, play, art exhibition, march etc. you may send press releases to either our Traffic Director or Production Director. Timeliness is essential, so please give us at least six weeks to act on press releases. Visit our contact page to find their information.

Posters:
You may also bring flyers to the station and post them with our permission. Please bring clearly printed flyers so they may be easily read over the air. Oversized posters tend to clutter up our windows, so please nothing over 8.5"x11"

Online events calendar:
You may email us at kcprpromotions@gmail.com with information for concerts. Please give at least two weeks notice and keep all correspondence relevant to the online calendar. Be sure to include names of performers, venue and address, show or door time, ticket price, age restrictions, promoters and any other pertinent information.

Interviews
Anyone wishing to be interviewed on KCPR may submit a press release along with an inquiry to the Program Director or News Director. If KCPR chooses to interview you, you will be contacted by one of our news anchors to schedule an interview for one of our daily news broadcasts.

Sponsorship:
If you wish to sponsor our station in return for airtime, you may visit our underwriting page for more information.
