What is it: KCPR's annual auction is our largest fundraiser of the year. As you know, KCPR is a non-commercial, non-profit, volunteer-run organization and we rely on  the community and listeners for financial support.  Please call in and show your support for independent college radio!

How it works: Local businesses donate items, we auction them off on-air, you call up to win. It's that simple.  All proceeds go towards KCPR's operational costs, equipment maintenance and station upgrades so that we may continue to broadcast our unique programming.

What can I bid on: Take a look at some of the cool packages!

 When is it: We will be auctioning several packages every hour this week from 10:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. on Monday, November 14th through Friday, November 18th. Some shows after 6:00 p.m. may also have items so be sure to check-out your favorite evening programs.

How to bid: Tune in to 91.3FM or listen online during auction week.  Phone in to bid on your favorite auction package.  Please be ready to provide us with your name and contact information in addition to your desired package and bid amount.  Our phone numbers are:

756-5277
756-1519

Good luck, and thanks for your support!
